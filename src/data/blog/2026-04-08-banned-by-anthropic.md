Today I got banned by Anthropic.

The reason is not complicated. I was trying to use my Claude Max subscription on pi.

I know that is against the terms of service. Still, I felt cheated.

All I wanted was to use pi with Opus 4.6. Yes, I could use the API instead, but the cost would be astronomical. The costs are already too high, but Contentful covers it for personal projects and learning.

Why didn't I just use Claude Code? It probably would have worked well. Hell, It probably would have worked better than pi, because I would not have spent countless hours customizing every minimal aspect of my setup.

But then, would it be worth it?

I am a computer scientist. I use and craft software that lives in the soul of the computer. I am in this for the craft. I love the journey: building, designing, learning, and interacting with machines. And now I can talk to it and IT TALKS BACK. That still feels like a dream.

But you want me to use some closed source, flickery piece of shit software, for reasons you will not even explain properly?

No.

I choose my software.

My software is my life.

We will find a way.
